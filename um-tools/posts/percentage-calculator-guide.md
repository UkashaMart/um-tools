# Percentage Calculator — Real Life Uses

Percentages are everywhere in daily life — shopping discounts, exam results, tax calculations, salary increases and profit/loss calculations. Our free percentage calculator handles all these instantly.

## What is a Percentage?

A percentage is a number expressed as a fraction of 100. The symbol % means "per hundred."

Example: 75% means 75 out of every 100.

## How to Calculate Percentage — 3 Methods

### Method 1: Find X% of a Number
Formula: Result = (Percentage / 100) × Number

Example: What is 18% of 50,000?
= (18 / 100) × 50,000 = 9,000

Use this for: GST, VAT, discount amounts, commission

### Method 2: What Percentage is A of B?
Formula: Percentage = (A / B) × 100

Example: What percentage is 450 out of 600?
= (450 / 600) × 100 = 75%

Use this for: Exam marks, completion rates, market share

### Method 3: Percentage Change
Formula: Change% = ((New - Old) / Old) × 100

Example: Price changed from 1,000 to 1,200
= ((1,200 - 1,000) / 1,000) × 100 = 20% increase

Use this for: Salary increase, price change, profit/loss

## Discount Calculator

Shopping discounts are the most common percentage use:

Original Price: PKR 5,000
Discount: 30%
Discount Amount: 5,000 × 30% = PKR 1,500
Final Price: 5,000 - 1,500 = PKR 3,500
