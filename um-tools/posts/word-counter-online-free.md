## Understanding Reading Time

Reading time is calculated based on an average adult reading speed of **200 words per minute**. This is a widely used standard for general content. Academic or technical content may take longer to read depending on complexity.

For example:
- 500 words → approximately 2–3 minutes
- 1000 words → approximately 5 minutes
- 2000 words → approximately 10 minutes
