# Unit Converter Online Free

Our free online unit converter lets you convert between hundreds of units across 7 categories: length, weight, temperature, speed, area, volume and digital data.

## Length Converter

Converting length units is one of the most common needs:

- **Kilometers to Miles** — 1 km = 0.621 miles
- **Miles to Kilometers** — 1 mile = 1.609 km
- **Feet to Meters** — 1 foot = 0.3048 meters
- **Inches to Centimeters** — 1 inch = 2.54 cm
- **Meters to Feet** — 1 meter = 3.281 feet

## Weight Converter

- **Kilograms to Pounds** — 1 kg = 2.205 lbs
- **Pounds to Kilograms** — 1 lb = 0.453 kg
- **Grams to Ounces** — 1 gram = 0.0353 oz
- **Tonnes to Kilograms** — 1 tonne = 1000 kg

## Temperature Converter

Temperature conversion is essential for cooking, science and weather:

- **Celsius to Fahrenheit** — F = (C × 9/5) + 32
- **Fahrenheit to Celsius** — C = (F - 32) × 5/9
- **Celsius to Kelvin** — K = C + 273.15

Common conversions:
- 0°C = 32°F (freezing point)
- 100°C = 212°F (boiling point)
- 37°C = 98.6°F (body temperature)
- 20°C = 68°F (room temperature)

## Speed Converter

- **mph to km/h** — 1 mph = 1.609 km/h
- **Knots to km/h** — 1 knot = 1.852 km/h
- **m/s to km/h** — 1 m/s = 3.6 km/h
