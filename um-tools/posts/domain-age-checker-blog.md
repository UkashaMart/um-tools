# Free Domain Age Checker — Registration Date Lookup

Want to know how old a domain is before buying it, or researching a website you came across? Our free domain age checker pulls registration data directly from official domain registries.

## What This Tool Shows

- **Domain age** in years and days
- **Registration date** — when the domain was first created
- **Expiration date** — when the current registration ends
- **Last updated date** — most recent record change
- **Registrar** — the company managing the domain
- **Nameservers** — DNS servers the domain uses

## Why Domain Age Matters

### Trust and Credibility
Older, established domains are often perceived as more trustworthy than brand new ones, both by users and some security systems.

### Buying Domains
Before purchasing an expired or aged domain, checking its registration history helps you understand what you're buying.

### Email Deliverability
Aged domains generally have better email sending reputation than newly registered ones, which are more frequently associated with spam.

### Security Research
Cybersecurity professionals often flag very recently registered domains for additional scrutiny, since they're commonly used in phishing campaigns.
