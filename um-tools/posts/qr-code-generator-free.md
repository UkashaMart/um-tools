# QR Code Generator Free Online

QR codes aaj kal har jagah hain — restaurants, shops, business cards, aur advertisements mein. Apna QR code banana ab bilkul free aur easy hai!

## QR Code Kya Hota Hai?

QR (Quick Response) code ek 2D barcode hai jo mobile camera se scan karne par kisi bhi URL, text ya information par redirect karta hai.

## QR Code Uses

### Business ke liye
- Website URL share karna
- WhatsApp Business link
- Online menu (restaurants)
- Payment link (EasyPaisa, JazzCash)

### Personal Use
- WiFi password share karna
- Contact information share karna
- Social media profiles

### Marketing
- Product packaging
- Business cards
- Flyers aur banners
- Email signatures

## Hamara QR Generator Features

- Koi bhi text ya URL enter karo
- 3 sizes available: 128px, 200px, 300px
- PNG mein download karo
- Bilkul free, no signup

## QR Code Tips

1. High contrast colors use karein (dark on light)
2. Minimum 2cm x 2cm size rakhein print mein
3. Test karein scan hota hai ya nahi
4. URL short karein (bit.ly) lamba URL ke liye
