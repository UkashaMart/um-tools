# QR Code Generator — Complete Business Guide 2026

QR codes are everywhere in 2026 — restaurants, shops, business cards, and advertisements. Creating your own QR code is now free and takes just seconds.

## What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a 2D barcode that smartphones can scan instantly. It was invented by Denso Wave in 1994 for tracking car parts, but today it is used for everything from websites to payments.

## Types of QR Codes

### URL QR Code
Link directly to your website, social media profile or any web page.

### WhatsApp QR Code
Create a QR code that opens a WhatsApp chat with your business number.
Format: https://wa.me/923001234567

### Payment QR Code
Link to EasyPaisa, JazzCash, Raast or any payment platform.

### WiFi QR Code
Let guests connect to your WiFi by scanning a QR code — no password typing needed.
Format: WIFI:T:WPA;S:NetworkName;P:Password;;

### vCard QR Code
Share your complete contact information with one scan.

## QR Code Best Practices

### Size Matters
- Minimum size for scanning: 2cm x 2cm
- For print materials: at least 3cm x 3cm
- For billboards: much larger depending on viewing distance

### Contrast is Key
Always use dark QR codes on light backgrounds. Never use low contrast colors — the scanner needs clear contrast to read the code.

### Always Test Before Printing
Scan your QR code with multiple phones before printing. What works on iPhone may behave differently on Android.

### Use a Short URL
Long URLs create complex QR codes that are harder to scan. Use a URL shortener like bit.ly first, then create the QR code.
