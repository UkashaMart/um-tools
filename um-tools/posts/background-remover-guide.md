# How to Remove a Background from Any Image for Free

Removing a background used to mean opening Photoshop and carefully tracing around a subject by hand. AI has changed that completely.

## How AI Background Removal Works

Modern background removal tools use a trained image segmentation model that identifies the main subject in a photo and separates it from everything behind it, pixel by pixel. The result is a PNG image with a transparent background that can be placed on any new background.

## Why Browser-Based Processing Matters

Many background removal tools upload your photo to a server to process it. Our tool works differently - it downloads a small AI model to your browser once, then processes every image locally on your own device. Your photos never get uploaded anywhere, which matters if you are working with private product photos, client images, or personal photos.

## Best Results Tips

- Use photos with good lighting and a clear subject
- Avoid backgrounds with colors very similar to your subject
- Higher resolution photos generally give cleaner edges
