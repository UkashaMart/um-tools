# Pakistan Income Tax Calculator 2025-26

FBR (Federal Board of Revenue) har saal tax slabs update karta hai. 2025-26 ke liye naye slabs yahan hain.

## FBR Income Tax Slabs 2025-26

| Annual Income | Tax Rate |
|---------------|----------|
| 0 – 6 Lakh | 0% (Tax Free) |
| 6L – 12L | 5% on excess over 6L |
| 12L – 22L | Rs. 30,000 + 15% on excess |
| 22L – 32L | Rs. 1,80,000 + 25% on excess |
| 32L – 41L | Rs. 4,30,000 + 30% on excess |
| 41L+ | Rs. 7,00,000 + 35% on excess |
