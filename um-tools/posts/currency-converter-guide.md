## How Exchange Rates Work

Exchange rates fluctuate constantly based on:
- **Supply and demand** for each currency
- **Central bank policies** (like SBP in Pakistan)
- **Inflation rates** in each country
- **Political stability** and economic news
- **Trade balances** between countries
