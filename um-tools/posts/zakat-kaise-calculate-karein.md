# Zakat Kaise Calculate Karein

Zakat Islam ke 5 arkan mein se ek hai. Har Muslim jo Nisab se zyada maal ka malik ho, us par zakat farz hai.

## Nisab kya hai?

Nisab woh minimum amount hai jis par zakat wajib hoti hai:
- **Gold:** 87.48 gram
- **Silver:** 612.36 gram
- **Cash/Savings:** Silver ki qeemat ke barabar

## Zakat ka Nisab 2024

Pakistan mein 2024 mein Nisab approximately **PKR 87,480** hai.

## Calculation ka Tarika

1. Apni total savings calculate karo
2. Debt (qarz) minus karo
3. Agar baqi amount Nisab se zyada ho aur ek saal guzar gaya ho
4. Total ka **2.5%** Zakat hai

## Example

- Total Savings: PKR 500,000
- Nisab: PKR 87,480
- 500,000 > 87,480 ✓ (Zakat Wajib)
- Zakat = 500,000 × 2.5% = **PKR 12,500**
