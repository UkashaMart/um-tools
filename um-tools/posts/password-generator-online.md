# Password Generator Online — Create Strong Passwords Instantly

Weak passwords are the number one cause of hacked accounts. Our free password generator creates strong, secure passwords in one click.

## What Makes a Strong Password?

A strong password has:
- At least 12 characters
- Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
- Numbers included
- Special symbols like !@#$

Example of a weak password: `john1990`
Example of a strong password: `Kx9#mP2vQr7!`

## Common Password Mistakes to Avoid

- Using your name or birth date
- Using the same password for multiple accounts
- Simple sequences like `123456` or `qwerty`
- Short passwords under 8 characters

## Tips for Managing Passwords

Use a password manager like Bitwarden (free) to store your passwords safely. Never write passwords on paper or save them in plain text files.
