# BMI Calculator — Complete Guide

BMI (Body Mass Index) ek simple formula hai jo aapki height aur weight se health estimate karta hai.

## BMI Formula

**BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m)**

## BMI Categories

| BMI Range | Category |
|-----------|----------|
| Under 18.5 | Underweight |
| 18.5 – 24.9 | Normal ✓ |
| 25 – 29.9 | Overweight |
| 30+ | Obese |

## Example

- Weight: 70 kg
- Height: 170 cm = 1.70 m
- BMI = 70 ÷ (1.70 × 1.70) = **24.2** (Normal ✓)
