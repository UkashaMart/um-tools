# BMI Calculator — A Complete Guide to Body Mass Index

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a widely used, simple tool that estimates your body fat based on your height and weight. It is a quick way to screen for potential weight-related health issues.

## The BMI Formula

BMI is calculated using the following mathematical formula:

**BMI = Weight (kg) / [Height (m)]²**

## BMI Categories

Understanding where you fall on the spectrum can help you make informed decisions about your health and fitness goals:

| BMI Range | Category |
|-----------|----------|
| Below 18.5 | Underweight |
| 18.5 – 24.9 | Healthy Weight ✓ |
| 25.0 – 29.9 | Overweight |
| 30.0 and Above | Obese |

## A Practical Example

Let’s calculate the BMI for someone who weighs **70 kg** and is **170 cm (1.70 m)** tall:

1. **Square the height:** 1.70 × 1.70 = 2.89
2. **Divide weight by height squared:** 70 ÷ 2.89 = **24.2**
3. **Result:** A BMI of 24.2 falls within the **Healthy Weight** category.
