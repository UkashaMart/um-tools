# Tip Calculator — Calculate Tip and Split Bills Instantly

Going out with friends and confused about how much to tip? Our free tip calculator makes it simple.

## How to Calculate a Tip

The basic tip formula is simple:

**Tip Amount = Bill Total x Tip Percentage / 100**

For example: Bill is $50, tip is 18%
- Tip = 50 x 18 / 100 = $9
- Total = $50 + $9 = $59
