# Loan EMI Calculator Pakistan 2026

Ghar, car ya business ke liye loan lena cha rahe hain? Pehle monthly installment (EMI) calculate karein taake budget plan kar sakein.

## EMI Kya Hota Hai?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) woh fixed amount hai jo aap har mahine bank ko dete hain. Isme principal aur interest dono shamil hote hain.

## EMI Formula

EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / [(1+r)^n - 1]

Jahan:
- P = Principal (Loan Amount)
- r = Monthly Interest Rate
- n = Number of Months
