# Grade Calculator — Calculate Your GPA and Percentage Online

Worried about your grades? Our free grade calculator helps you instantly calculate your percentage, letter grade and GPA.

## How to Calculate Your Grade

The basic formula is:

**Percentage = (Marks Obtained / Total Marks) x 100**

Example: You scored 450 out of 600
- Percentage = (450 / 600) x 100 = 75%
- Letter Grade = B+
- GPA = 3.0

## What is Weighted Grade?

A weighted grade gives more importance to subjects with more credit hours. For example, a 4-credit subject affects your GPA more than a 1-credit subject.

Our grade calculator supports weighted grades — just enter the weight for each subject.
