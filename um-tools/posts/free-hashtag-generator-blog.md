## Hashtag Strategy Tips

### Mix Hashtag Sizes
Don't only use massive hashtags like #love or #instagood. Mix in niche, specific hashtags related directly to your content for better targeting.

### Avoid Banned Hashtags
Some hashtags get flagged by platforms and can actually reduce your reach. Stick to genuine, relevant tags.

### Rotate Your Hashtags
Using the exact same hashtags on every post can look spammy to algorithms. Rotate between similar sets.

### Place Them Naturally
You can put hashtags directly in your caption or in the first comment — both work equally well on Instagram.
