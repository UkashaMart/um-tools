# Free Website Speed Checker — Powered by Google PageSpeed Insights

A slow website costs you visitors, rankings, and sales. Our free website speed checker gives you an instant, accurate report on exactly how your site performs.

## What This Tool Checks

- **Performance Score** — overall speed rating from 0 to 100
- **SEO Score** — how search-engine friendly your page is
- **Accessibility Score** — how usable your site is for all visitors
- **Best Practices Score** — general web development quality
- **Core Web Vitals** — FCP, LCP, TBT, CLS, and Speed Index

## Why This Matters for Your Website

### Search Rankings
Google explicitly uses Core Web Vitals as a ranking signal. A faster site has a real chance at ranking higher.

### User Experience
Studies show visitors expect pages to load in under 3 seconds. Slower sites see dramatically higher bounce rates.

### Conversions
Even a one-second delay in load time can reduce mobile conversions significantly.

## How to Use This Tool

1. Enter your website URL (single check) or up to 5 URLs (bulk check)
2. Choose Mobile or Desktop testing
3. Click Check Speed
4. Review your scores and Core Web Vitals metrics

| 0-49 | Poor — significant issues present |
